## Synthetic Data Notice

All data generated by this pipeline — including but not limited to customer
profiles, representative personas, call records, store visit records, account
ledgers, and transcripts — is **entirely synthetic**.

- No real customer data was used at any stage.
- No real employee data was used at any stage.
- No real transaction, account, or billing records were used at any stage.
- All names, identifiers, phone numbers, account numbers, and personal
  attributes are randomly generated and do not correspond to any real individual
  or account.

Any resemblance to real persons, customers, employees, or accounts is
coincidental and unintentional.

## Research Use

This pipeline and its outputs are provided for research, governance framework
development, and educational purposes. The synthetic data is designed to model
realistic operational dynamics and is not intended to represent the actual
performance, operations, or practices of any specific company or organization.

Companion paper: Aulabaugh, G. (2026). *The hidden cost of measurement: Why
